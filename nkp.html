On Canal St Martin: "Comptoir Général" or Point Ephémère
They're both bars but also concert venues at night, so you could check out the events and see if anything cool's happening

off Concorde metro station: Le Maxim's, it's a good cheap bar

In the 6th quarter:
Chez Georges also a cheap bar, you can buy a bottle of wine for 20 euros (cheap) but also glasses obvi.

near Montmartre:
Le Fourmi (bar)

L'Estaminet is a nice restaurant that isn't too expensive and serves typical French cuisine
L'Autre Cafe is a cute bar/cafe/restaurant
Le Nun's Cafe is another bar that's decorated with rando vintage/garage sale furniture items, kinda fun
